Who Are We?

The Riverside Art Center is a not-for-profit Arts Council, with an eleven member Board of Trustees. We are incorporated, and have a Federal Tax ID. We have obtained a 501(c)(3) Status with the State of Ohio, as well as received grants from Ohio Arts Council, who remain supporters. Riverside strives to promote all forms of artistic expression, with focus on the visual arts.

We are a community minded organization, offering innovative art events and activities for all ages. We are family oriented and open to all surrounding communities. The center is dedicated to providing a wide range of programs that are not only entertaining and educational, but also benefit participants through the satisfaction of their personal accomplishments! We give people a reason to be excited about the Arts!

Please Visit the Art Center to Learn More!
"We are a home for creative minds and improve lives through education, fellowship and personal accomplishment."
What do we have to offer?

RAC promotes the Arts through programs and events held at the center and surrounding areas. We offer classes, lectures and demonstrations, as well as participating in several community-minded events each year. The center is a place to display and sell members’ art and to attend and hold classes. It is also available to rent as part of your membership! We are open to citizens of all ages, and provide uplifing programs for children, the elderly and the handicapped.

We want to help improve the image of our downtown community by being a positive and healthy influence on the general public. We provide activities which attract people to Wapakoneta, which in turn, creates more commerce for the city.
