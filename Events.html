EVENTS

We proudly host a variety of art events designed to inspire and connect our creative community. Our events include interactive workshops led by renowned artists and vibrant art fairs that bring together art enthusiasts and creators. These events provide unique opportunities for learning, networking, and celebrating the diverse world of art. Join us to experience the joy of creativity, discover new techniques, and be part of a dynamic artistic community.

CLASSES

Discover the artist within at our engaging art classes, designed for all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner eager to learn the basics or an experienced artist looking to refine your technique, our courses offer personalized instruction, inspiring projects, and a supportive community to help you unleash your creativity and achieve your artistic goals.

EXHIBIT

we proudly host captivating exhibits that showcase the incredible talents of our community, providing a platform for artists to display their work and gain recognition. Our exhibits are a celebration of creativity, featuring a diverse range of artistic styles and mediums. Furthermore, we offer a selection of unique merchandise, including prints, handcrafted items, and art supplies, all designed to inspire and support your creative journey. Whether you're looking to admire, acquire, or create, our space is dedicated to nurturing and celebrating the artistic spirit.

The Moon Market

The Moon Market, part of the Summer Moon Festival, offers a diverse range of products beneath the summer moon's glow. From artisan crafts to celestial-themed jewelry, it's a celebration of community, creativity, and sustainability, where attendees can explore, connect, and indulge in the magic of the cosmos.
