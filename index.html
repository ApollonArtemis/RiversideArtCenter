Riverside Arts Center

A community space that promotes visual arts. It offers art exhibitions, workshops, and classes for all ages and skill levels. The center aims to foster creativity and an appreciation for the arts in the local community and beyond.

MISSION STATEMENT

The Riverside Art Center (RAC) is a non-profit arts corporation dedicated to the promotion of fine art. RAC serves the greater Wapakoneta area, providing art related, educational opportunities through classes, lectures and demonstrations. RAC also provides a venue for local artists to work, display and sell their artwork. The RAC provides these opportunities for all persons, regardless of age, social status, gender/gender orientation, politics, religious affiliation

About

The Riverside Art Center is a regional anchor for creative practice and learning. Home to galleries, artist studios, and a school that showcases the art of our time, RAC serves as a hub for artistic exploration and community engagement. Since 1993, the Riverside Arts Center (RAC) has been providing Riverside, its surrounding suburbs and the city of Chicago with vital arts programming. We host exhibitions, arts classes, special events, and celebrations of creativity. Located in the heart of Historic Riverside, IL, the RAC includes the Freeark Gallery + Sculpture Garden, The FlexSpace, and a variety of studio spaces, including a renowned clay studio that has a full range of electric, raku and stoneware kilns.
